#0001: Yap #1 - Respiration
11/15/2025

My first real blog will be the first in a series known as "Yaps". I'll basically just explain some topic I learned about recently or that I find interesting. Keep in mind that this information is just off the top of my head, so take it with a grain of salt. Today's will be cellular respiration.

Firstly, respiration has 2 types: aerobic & anaerobic. Aerobic respiration takes place when your cells have plenty of O2, and produces around 40 ATP, which is, in short, a molecule your body uses for energy. Anaerobic respiration occurs when your cels lack oxygen, such as if your circulatory system malfunctions. It produces 4 ATP.
